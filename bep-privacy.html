English

Last updated: February 2026

General

BEP Hazırlama is developed by Dev Software. The application is designed to work fully offline.

All data created within the application is stored locally on the user's device. No data is transmitted to the developer or any external servers.

Data Collection

The BEP Hazırlama application does not collect, store, or share any personal data by the developer. All processing happens locally on the user's device.

Advertising Services (Google AdMob)

The app may display advertisements provided by Google AdMob. Google AdMob may automatically collect anonymous data such as device type, OS information, and ad interactions. This data is not accessed or stored by Dev Software.

Contact

If you have any questions or feedback regarding privacy, please contact us at: devsoftwaremail@gmail.com
