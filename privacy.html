Privacy Policy

Effective Date: February 14, 2026

Welcome to Dev Software. We respect your privacy and are committed to protecting the personal data of our users. This Privacy Policy explains how we handle information in our mobile applications and services.

1. Information We (Don't) Collect

Most of our applications are designed to function offline and do not require you to create an account. For these apps:

  • We do not collect personal information (name, email, phone number).
  • Data you enter into the app is stored locally on your device and is not transmitted to our servers.

2. Permissions

Our apps may request certain device permissions to function correctly, such as:

  • Storage: To read or save files (like PDF documents).
  • Camera: Only if required for the app's core feature (e.g., scanning).

You can revoke these permissions at any time through your device settings.

3. Third-Party Services

We may use third-party tools (like Google Play Services) for app stability and performance monitoring. These services may collect information used to identify your device type and crash logs to help us improve the software.

4. Data Security

Since most of your data is stored locally on your device, its security depends on your device's security measures. We recommend keeping your operating system updated.

5. Children's Privacy

Our applications do not knowingly collect any personal identifiable information from children under 13. If you are a parent and think your child provided us with information, please contact us immediately.

6. Changes to This Policy

We may update our Privacy Policy from time to time. Thus, you are advised to review this page periodically for any changes. Changes are effective immediately after they are posted on this page.

7. Contact Us

If you have any questions or suggestions about our Privacy Policy, do not hesitate to contact us at our contact page.
